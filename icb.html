ice cave blues is an audiovisual piece in 8 parts floating between gas clouds and hyperspace. while the pieces are experimental and improvised, they are grounded in textures that appeal to familiar sensations and emotions

ice cave blues was recorded in december 2020. the tools used were frequency modulation synthesis and free trial software. the visuals were produced with everyday iphone videos and 3d animations, processed in m4l
